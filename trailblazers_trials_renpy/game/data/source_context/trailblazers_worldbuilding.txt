TRAILBLAZERS WORLDBUILDING MASTER FILE
Timestamp: April 21, 2026 (America/New_York)

=====================================
SECTION 1: MAGIC SYSTEM
=====================================

Magic is the fundamental building block of all matter and energy.
- Cannot be created or destroyed
- Exists as quantum-like "magic atoms"
- Can take any form depending on influence

States of Magic:
1. Elemental Magic (common)
2. Pure White Magic (rare, pre-elemental)
3. Quantum Magic (foundational, governs reality)

Time Magic:
- Requires pure white magic + specific activation (poems/runes)
- Does NOT create timelines
- Only affects user + touched objects

Mana System:
- 3 pools: Head, Heart, Stomach
- Exhaustion leads to environmental absorption
- Overuse causes damage/aging

Environmental Interaction:
- Weather + mana = amplified effects
Example:
Heat + mana → fire tornadoes
Rain + mana → geysers

=====================================
SECTION 2: BIOLOGY
=====================================

All life is composed of magic atoms.

Reproduction:
- All races can reproduce together
- No hybrids
- Stronger gene dominates

Gene hierarchy:
Human < Elf < Orc < Beastman < Drysk < Dragon

Lifespans:
Humans: 170 (500+ magic)
Elves/Drysk: 480 (800+ magic)
Orcs/Beastmen: 320 (600+ magic)
Dragons: 1600–5000

Physical Traits:
Humans: balanced
Elves: tall/slender
Orcs: large/strong
Beastmen: small/aggressive
Drysk: reptilian
Dragons: pure mana beings

Limits:
- Limb regrowth possible (skill dependent)
- No permanent species change (except dragons temporarily)
- No natural immortality
- Magic can create life but not consciousness

Soul System:
- Soul > Body
- Stronger soul dominates
- Oren = dual soul (special case)

Creature Tiers:
Hiccup → Misfortune → Disaster → Calamity
