TRAILBLAZERS - OREN TIMELINE ANCHOR FILE
Timestamp: April 21, 2026 (America/New_York)

PURPOSE:
This file defines the chronological life events, status, and narrative positioning of Oren.
This timeline acts as the PRIMARY reference for all story generation and gameplay logic involving Oren.

IDENTITY:
- Name: Oren
- Role: Primary protagonist
- Status: Human host with unknown internal entity (Jötunn)
- Classification (hidden): Potential Calamity-level threat

PHASE 0 - PRE-BIRTH EVENT (HIDDEN):
- Dragon entity Jötunn attacks Severence headquarters.
- Jötunn is defeated by combined Severence forces.
- Jötunn's soul is extracted and forcibly implanted into a human infant.
- Two souls now exist in one body: original human soul Oren and dragon soul Jötunn.
- This event is completely unknown to the world, Dravrah, kingdoms, and public history.

PHASE 1 - CHILDHOOD (UNKNOWN HISTORY):
- Oren grows up as a normal human child.
- Jötunn exists silently within him.
- Oren is unaware of Jötunn and has no understanding of his condition.
- Only one unknown figure knows the truth; do not reference or reveal this figure at this stage.

PHASE 2 - DRAVRAH ENROLLMENT:
- Oren enrolls in Dravrah College.
- He is seen as a normal student with no known abnormalities.
- Jötunn remains inactive or unrecognized.

PHASE 3 - DRAVRAH INCIDENT:
- A Severence-created, mutated Kraken of extreme scale attacks Dravrah Academy.
- The Kraken is comparable to a skyscraper in scale.
- Its hidden goal is eliminating noble bloodline students.
- Publicly, the attack is believed to be a natural mana anomaly.
- Oren cannot defeat the enemy normally and intentionally loses consciousness.
- Jötunn takes control of the body.
- A hybrid dragon-human form manifests and destroys the Kraken, saving Dravrah.
- Witnesses see Oren transform into a dragon-like entity with extreme destructive power.
- Severence involvement, the Kraken's true nature, and Jötunn's existence remain hidden from all known factions, including Dravrah leadership.

PHASE 4 - POST-INCIDENT CLASSIFICATION:
- Oren is not executed because he saved Dravrah.
- Authorities classify him as unstable, dangerous, and a potential Calamity-level threat.
- Oren is placed under observation and monitored over time.

PHASE 5 - GRADUATION AND ASSIGNMENT:
- A few months after the Dravrah incident, Oren graduates from Dravrah.
- Oren is placed under Master Embrum as a squire under Highguard supervision.
- The assignment provides mentorship, monitoring, containment, and long-term threat evaluation.

PHASE 6 - CURRENT STORY STATE (HEART OF FIRE):
- Location: Anozira Village.
- Situation: Village suffers drought caused by an unknown magical creature.
- Oren assists Embrum as a squire and participates in field missions.
- Oren is depressed, isolated, and distrustful of himself.

JÖTUNN INTERACTION STATE:
- Jötunn actively speaks within Oren's mind.
- Jötunn claims he is a dragon, was sealed, and will regain his body.
- Oren believes the voice is his own mind and thinks he may be going insane.
- Oren rejects Jötunn's claims because dragons have been sealed for 2226 years and there is no living proof of their existence.

CONTROL RULES:
- Oren normally has full control of the body.
- Jötunn can speak, influence thoughts, and apply emotional pressure.
- Jötunn takes control temporarily when Oren loses consciousness.

KNOWLEDGE RESTRICTIONS:
- Oren does not know Severence exists, that Jötunn is real, that his body contains two souls, or the origin of his power.
- The public does not know the true cause of the Dravrah incident or the nature of Oren's transformation.
- Dravrah leadership does not know Severence involvement, the true nature of the Kraken, or Jötunn's existence.
- Embrum does not know Jötunn's true identity or soul-binding details.
- Truth holders are Severence and one unknown individual who must not be revealed.

NARRATIVE FUNCTION:
- Oren represents internal vs external conflict, power vs control, and ignorance vs truth.
- Story progression relies on gradual revelation, increasing Jötunn influence, and rising suspicion from others.
