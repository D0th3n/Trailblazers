DRAVRAH INCIDENT UPDATE

- A Severence-created, mutated Kraken of extreme scale attacked Dravrah Academy.
- The Kraken was comparable to a skyscraper in size.
- Its hidden goal was eliminating noble bloodline students.
- The Kraken's origin is completely unknown to the public.
- Public belief frames the attack as a natural mana anomaly.
- During the battle, Oren intentionally lost consciousness because he could not defeat the Kraken normally.
- Jötunn took control of Oren's body.
- A hybrid dragon-human transformation manifested.
- The transformed Oren/Jötunn destroyed the Kraken and saved Dravrah Academy.
- Witnesses observed a human transforming into a dragon-like being and unleashing extreme destructive power.
- Oren was classified as a potential Calamity-level threat because of this transformation and power.
- Severence involvement, the true nature of the Kraken, and Jötunn's existence remain completely hidden from all known factions, including Dravrah leadership.
- Oren interprets Jötunn's voice as his own thoughts and believes he may be mentally unstable.
- Oren rejects Jötunn's claims because dragons have been sealed for over two thousand years and are considered effectively nonexistent.
