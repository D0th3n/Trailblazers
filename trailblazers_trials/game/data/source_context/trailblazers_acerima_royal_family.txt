TRAILBLAZERS — ACERIMA ROYAL FAMILY
Timestamp: April 24, 2026 (America/New_York)

PURPOSE:
This file defines the current royal family of Acerima, its bloodline traits, allied houses, and hidden truths relevant to future story expansion.

=====================================
FOUNDING FIGURES
=====================================

JYRA
- Daughter of the Highest Order
- First Queen of Acerima
- Source of Divine Qana authority in the bloodline
- Rarely appears, but may intervene in guidance-heavy or emotionally critical moments
- Eventually ascended back to the throne room of the Highest Order

ARTHUR
- Dragon of Healing Light
- Hidden beneath the capital
- Free to move, but chooses secrecy
- Deeply caring father focused on his children
- Source of divine healing traits in the royal bloodline
- Protected by secrecy and binding systems

=====================================
SECOND GENERATION
=====================================

AURE
- Son of Jyra and Arthur
- Father of Jysca and Arthur II
- Married Selena Ardwyn
- Currently captured by the Highest Order
- Being studied for Divine Qana and healing properties
- Officially missing from the public-facing royal story

JYANA
- Daughter of Jyra and Arthur
- Current Queen of Acerima
- Married Luther Vey
- Rules with council support
- Enforced secrecy over Arthur using binding spell
- Maintains the current structure of royal authority

=====================================
ROYAL SPOUSES AND ALLIED HOUSES
=====================================

SELENA ARDWYN
- Wife of Aure
- Member of House Ardwyn
- Light-state Qana affinity
- Brings healing, knowledge, ecosystem, and governance lineage into the royal bloodline

LUTHER VEY
- Husband of Jyana
- Son of Brenic Vey
- Swordmaster
- Represents the strength and warrior lineage of House Vey

=====================================
THIRD GENERATION
=====================================

JYSCA
- Daughter of Aure and Selena
- Politically driven
- Sees herself as a sacred daughter of Jyra
- Feels pressure to become future queen
- Deeply affected by Aure's disappearance

RIN
- Son of Jyana and Luther
- Twin of Ruther
- Serious and disciplined
- Trains in the Elven Kingdom under his godfather
- Strong warrior mindset

RUTHER
- Son of Jyana and Luther
- Twin of Rin
- Lighthearted and carefree
- Travels with Rin
- Less burdened by responsibility

ARTHUR II — EMBRUM
- Son of Aure and Selena
- Travels under the alias "Embrum"
- Member of the Highguard
- Searching for his father Aure
- Travels with Oren for dual reasons:
  - the official reason: supervise and field-test Oren under Highguard duty
  - the hidden personal reason: continue searching for Aure
- Hides divine healing abilities
- Uses fire Qana in combat instead of openly revealing his healing lineage

=====================================
BLOODLINE TRAITS
=====================================

CORE BLOODLINE SOURCES

The royal bloodline is the union of:
- Jyra's Divine Qana authority
- Arthur's healing dragon lineage
- House Ardwyn's healing and knowledge tradition
- House Vey's strength and combat legacy

FEMALE DESCENDANTS
- Direct command over Divine Qana
- Can create constructs such as hands, barriers, guardians, and weapons
- Tend toward overt Divine Qana authority and front-line construct fighting

MALE DESCENDANTS
- Divine healing through Arthur's lineage
- Regeneration and restoration traits
- Healing often occurs naturally rather than through complex conscious control

=====================================
NOBLE HOUSES
=====================================

HOUSE VEY
- Origin: warrior tribe from the dragon-rule era
- Roles: knights, heavy workers, guild masters, military leaders, enforcement specialists
- Identity: strength, combat, enforcement, endurance

HOUSE ARDWYN
- Origin: healers and ecosystem specialists
- Roles: doctors, priests, potion makers, judges, information officials, ecosystem experts
- Identity: healing, knowledge, law, preservation

IN-LAW SIGNIFICANCE
- House Vey adds strength and protection to the royal line
- House Ardwyn adds healing, knowledge, and governance
- Together they complete the royal bloodline as force plus preservation

=====================================
CURRENT STRUCTURE
=====================================

- Jyana is the current Queen of Acerima
- Aure is missing to the public, but secretly captured by the Highest Order
- Arthur remains hidden beneath the capital
- Jyra rarely intervenes
- Jysca is being shaped for future rule
- Rin and Ruther train in the Elven Kingdom
- Arthur II operates in the field as "Embrum"

=====================================
CORE TRUTH
=====================================

The royal family is not just a political dynasty.
It is a converged bloodline of:
- Divine authority
- dragon healing power
- human strength
- human knowledge

Its public legitimacy, hidden fractures, and protected secrets should shape future Acerima stories.
