TRAILBLAZERS — CAC & TAA SYSTEMS

Central Arcane Commission (CAC):
Regulates all new Qana technology such as Qana recyclers.
Approves, tests, and maintains devices.
Does not control traditional spellcasting.
Enforcement handled by Highguard and local authorities.
Relationship with Dravrah: tense, due to new vs old systems.

Teleportation Authority Association (TAA):
Independent, profit-driven organization.
Controls teleportation towers and global travel network.
Access requires payment + TAA-issued ID.
No class restrictions, but bans are possible.

Teleportation:
Physical body travels through stabilized Qana beam.
Risks include Qana burn based on distance and frequency.

Trailblazers:
Repair broken routes and checkpoints.
High risk, high reward system tied to TAA failures.
