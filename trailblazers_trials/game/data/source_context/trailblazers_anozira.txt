TRAILBLAZERS — ANOZIRA VILLAGE CONTEXT FILE
Timestamp: April 21, 2026 (America/New_York)

PURPOSE:
Defines the full ecological, economic, and narrative context of Anozira Village.
This file acts as the primary reference for all systems, dialogue, and events occurring in Anozira.

=====================================
LOCATION & REGIONAL IMPORTANCE
=====================================

Name: Anozira Village
Region: Human Kingdom (Acerima)
Type: Agricultural village

Nearest Major Hub:
- The Port of Eldran

PORT OF ELDRAN:
- Coastal trade hub
- Founded by Eldran (wealthy trade magnate)
- Major export center for kingdom goods
- Connects Acerima to overseas markets

Primary Trade Route:
Anozira → Port of Eldran → Overseas (Elven Kingdom)

=====================================
ECONOMIC ROLE
=====================================

Anozira is a high-quality nairn production village.

PRIMARY EXPORT:
- Nairn (grain/feed)

Key Trait:
- Anozira nairn is known to produce:
  - faster livestock growth
  - heavier Brumel mass gain

This makes it highly valuable for:
- livestock farmers
- trade markets
- export economies

=====================================
INTERNATIONAL TRADE CONNECTION
=====================================

Primary External Buyer:
- Elven Kingdom (across the sea)

Reason:
- Redwood environments limit natural agriculture
- Elves rely on imported feed for livestock

Trade Flow:
Anozira → Port of Eldran → Ships → Elven Kingdom

Impact:
- Anozira directly supports elven livestock survival
- Port of Eldran profits heavily from this trade

=====================================
CURRENT CRISIS (HEART OF FIRE ARC)
=====================================

Condition:
- Severe drought

Effects:
- Srag fields drying and dying
- Nairn production collapsing
- Livestock feed shortages increasing

Economic Consequences:
- Feed prices rising across the region
- Trade disruption at Port of Eldran
- Pressure from merchants and exporters
