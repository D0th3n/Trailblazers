TRAILBLAZERS — QANA SYSTEM
Timestamp: April 22, 2026 (America/New_York)

PURPOSE:
This file replaces the older "mana/magic" framing with Qana, a more scientific quantum-energy model for the Trailblazers world.

TERMINOLOGY LOCK:
- Qana is the universal quantum-energy field/substrate underlying matter, energy, biology, thought-linked spell expression, and elemental behavior.
- Spells are shaped outputs, programmed expressions, or local collapses of Qana.
- Qana quanta are the particle-like packets or excitations of the Qana field.
- Older words like "mana" and "magic" should be treated as deprecated, regional slang, religious language, or legacy terminology unless intentionally used.
- Standard narration and game systems should use Qana.

REAL QUANTUM INSPIRATION, WORLD VERSION:
- Real particles are better understood as excitations of fields, not tiny hard marbles.
- Qana follows the same feel: the Qana field fills reality, while Qana quanta appear where the field is excited, bound, shaped, or collapsed.
- Wave-like Qana is diffuse, ambient, and possibility-rich.
- Particle-like Qana is localized, measurable, and able to interact physically.
- A spell works by forcing nearby Qana from possibility into one stable expression.

QANA STATES:
1. Field state:
   - Diffuse, ambient, uncommitted Qana.
   - Exists in air, matter, living bodies, weather, ore, soil, and tools.

2. Bound state:
   - Qana stabilized into matter, biology, ore, plants, flesh, relics, or devices.

3. Expressed state:
   - Active spell output such as heat, water, lightning, pressure, motion, growth, light, or healing.

4. Collapsed state:
   - A resolved spell result that has become stable enough to behave as physical reality.

CORE RULES:
- Qana cannot be created or destroyed; it changes state, pattern, density, and expression.
- Qana prefers stable patterns and falls into the strongest nearby influence.
- Dominant influences include heat, pressure, water, emotion, memory, language, runes, tool geometry, light, death, and environmental conditions.
- Living minds act as patterning engines that impose structure on Qana.
- Spells fail when intent, structure, and available Qana do not collapse into the same stable expression.
- Skilled spellcasters can hold unresolved Qana longer and collapse it more precisely.

ELEMENTS:
- Elements are not separate supernatural types.
- Elements are common stable attractor states of Qana.
- Fire is heat-dominant Qana collapse.
- Water is cohesion/flow-dominant Qana collapse.
- Earth is pressure/binding-dominant Qana collapse.
- Lightning is discharge/voltage-dominant Qana collapse.
- Flora is growth-pattern-dominant Qana collapse.

THREE QANA POOLS:
- Stomach pool: biological Qana reserve, fed by food, digestion, rest, and bodily health.
- Heart pool: emotional Qana reserve, fed by feeling, memory, motivation, grief, love, fear, and resolve.
- Head pool: cognitive Qana reserve, fed by meditation, stillness, focus, and deliberate mental structuring.

QANA ECOLOGY:
- Qana can be both an energy field and a biological requirement depending on the organism.
- Humans rely primarily on oxygen respiration and replenish Qana indirectly through the stomach, heart, and head pools.
- Qana creatures are pure field-dependent lifeforms that absorb ambient Qana directly through their bodies instead of breathing oxygen.
- Qana recyclers reduce ambient Qana density, stabilize weather, and make areas uninhabitable for Qana-dependent creatures.
- Human settlements are engineered safe zones maintained by recycler infrastructure.
- Wild zones are high-Qana environments where Qana creatures, elemental instability, and Cron risk are more common.
- A failed or sabotaged recycler can turn a safe zone back into creature-habitable territory.

SPELLCASTING LADDER:
- Beginner: needs tools, Ether Resin, full spell circles, chants, poems, or runes to hold Qana stable.
- Intermediate: can exude and shape Qana briefly without full external structure.
- Advanced: can complete partial spell structures because intent fills missing geometry.
- Master: can collapse a full spell expression with minimal gesture, phrase, or thought.

DRAGONS:
- Dragons are biological beings whose bodies and souls are natively stabilized around Pure Qana.
- They are not simply "made of old-style magic"; they are living Pure Qana biology.

CRONS:
- Crons are corpse-vessels where Qana collapses into one dominant elemental mode after exposure to extreme density and environmental influence.
- Crons are not conscious like dragons.
