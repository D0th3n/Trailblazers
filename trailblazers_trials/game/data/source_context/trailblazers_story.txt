TRAILBLAZERS STORY MASTER FILE
Timestamp: April 21, 2026 (America/New_York)

=====================================
SECTION 1: ORIGIN EVENTS
=====================================

The Highest Order (Author Entity):
- Supreme being controlling reality
- Exists outside normal magic system
- Creator-level authority

Jyra (Daughter of the Highest Order):
- Created from the rib of the Highest Order
- Sent to the world during the age of dragon rule
- Led the human revolt against dragons
- Became the first Queen of Acerima
- Later returned to the Highest Order's realm

Arthur (Dragon of Healing Light):
- Dragon who sided with humans
- Husband of Jyra
- Still alive
- Hidden beneath the human capital
- Ancestor of the royal bloodline

=====================================
SECTION 2: THE DRAGON AGE & REVOLT
=====================================

- Dragons ruled the world as divine beings
- Drysk acted as their noble intermediaries
- Lower races (humans, elves, orcs, beastmen) were oppressed

The Revolt:
- Led by Jyra
- Supported by Arthur
- Resulted in:
  - downfall of dragon rule
  - sealing of dragons

=====================================
SECTION 3: CURRENT WORLD STATE
=====================================

- Dragons are sealed away
- Most races believe dragons are gone or mythical
- Drysk survivors operate in secret
- Severance operates as a hidden faction
- Magic is regulated (especially in human kingdoms)

=====================================
SECTION 4: OREN & JÖTUNN
=====================================

Original State:
- Oren was born human
- Jötunn was a powerful dragon

Event:
- Jötunn attacked Severence headquarters to retrieve stolen artifact

Defeat:
- Jötunn was overpowered by combined Severence forces
- Jötunn's soul extracted from dragon body

Curse:
- Jötunn's soul forced into Oren's heart
- Bound by Severence curse

Goal of Enemy:
- Kill Oren's body -> kill both Oren and Jötunn

Outcome:
- Oren escaped as a baby (unknown cause)

Current State:
- Human body
- Two souls:
  - Human Oren
  - Jötunn

Rules:
- Both souls die if body dies
- Jötunn's dragon soul is stronger but suppressed by curse
- Jötunn may take control if Oren loses consciousness

Motivation:
- Oren seeks answers and survival
- Jötunn wants freedom from Oren's heart and return to his dragon body
- Death Mage seeks to finish killing Jötunn through Oren

=====================================
SECTION 5: MAJOR ENTITIES
=====================================

Time Dragon:
- Exists across all time
- Can move freely through time
- Only being who knows time magic
- Lighthearted personality
- Created directly by Highest Order

Death Dragon:
- Rules the afterrealm
- Controls soul processing and rebirth
- Can preserve memory (rare cases)
- Emotionally neutral but observant
- Can astral project into overworld

Death Mage:
- Obsessed with Death Dragon
- Learned to observe afterrealm via soul anchoring
- Created soul transfer technique
- Uses hosts to remain alive
- Can suppress or remove host souls
- Created Oren's curse

=====================================
SECTION 6: THE SEVERANCE
=====================================

- Hidden organization
- Seeks to reshape reality through magic
- Possibly influenced by Drysk
- Uses forbidden magic

Capabilities:
- soul extraction
- soul transfer
- curse creation

=====================================
SECTION 7: AFTERLIFE TRUTH
=====================================

Actual System:
- Soul enters afterrealm
- Memories fade
- Rebirth occurs

Special Cases:
- Death Dragon can preserve memory
- Highest Order may intervene
- Strong emotional connections can restore temporary memory

=====================================
SECTION 8: GLOBAL EVENTS
=====================================

Fantasia (Wizard Games):
- Island near Dravrah
- Hosts global magical competition
- Rewards:
  - wealth
  - land
  - rare spells

Purpose:
- encourages travel
- creates global interaction
