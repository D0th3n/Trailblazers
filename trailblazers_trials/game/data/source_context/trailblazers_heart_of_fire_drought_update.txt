Timestamp: April 22, 2026 (America/New_York)

PURPOSE:
This file overrides the earlier Heart of Fire assumption that Ruzen is leaking fire-Qana or acting as a fire-Qana vent.

LOCKED DROUGHT CAUSE:
- Severence places a Titan-class fire golem deep within the Ruzen mines.
- The Titan charges itself by feeding on fire energy from Ruzen deposits and lava heat deep inside the mountain.
- While charging, the Titan superheats the surrounding mountain and ground.
- This heat reaches underground water reservoirs connected to Anozira's wells.
- The reservoirs evaporate or become steam-heavy, causing wells to run dry or release steam instead of usable water.
- The hot ground prevents young srag, nairn, and sinu from surviving because the soil is outside their environmental temperature range.

IMPORTANT CORRECTION:
- The drought is not caused by fire-Qana leaking into the fields.
- The mine is not "breathing" because it is alive.
- The practical visible clues should be hot earth, steam, dry wells, scalding buckets, overheated tools, and crops failing from soil heat.
- Ruzen mines remain the practical lead because they sit above/near the Titan's charging location.

EARLY CHARACTER UNDERSTANDING:
- Villagers do not know the Titan or Severence exist.
- Villagers should describe symptoms in grounded terms: steam from wells, warm water, cracked hot soil, tools too hot to hold, and mine heat increasing after shutdown.
- Embrum should suspect a deep geothermal/Qana heat source under Ruzen, not a named Titan or conspiracy.
- Oren/Jötunn can sense that something is consuming power below, but Oren distrusts that insight.
