TRAILBLAZERS — ELF NOBLE FAMILIES
Timestamp: May 4, 2026 (America/New_York)

PURPOSE:
This file defines the currently active noble-family structure of the Elven Kingdom, including the present ruling royal house, major elemental clan families, and key house-specific canon.

IMPORTANT CANON NOTE:
- This file overrides older elf-politics notes when there is a conflict about the current royal house, current Shogun family names, or named clan leaders.

=====================================
CORE CONCEPT
=====================================

- The Elven Kingdom is structured around elemental noble families and a female-led Shogunate.
- Whoever holds the Shogun seat becomes the current royal family.
- The current ruling royal house is House Ikazuchi.

=====================================
HOUSE IKAZUCHI — CURRENT ROYAL HOUSE
=====================================

Identity:
- Electric High Elf royal family
- tied to lightning discipline, elite movement, battlefield dominance, and Shogunate legitimacy

Current royal parents:
- Ikazuchi Tomoe
  - current female Shogun
  - co-creator of Raifu Step
- Ikazuchi Hayate
  - Wind Elf husband of Tomoe
  - co-creator of Raifu Step

Royal aunt:
- Ikazuchi Masako
  - older sister of Tomoe
  - terrifying trainer and godmother to Rin and Ruther
  - trusted by Queen Jyana

Children of Tomoe and Hayate:
- Ikazuchi Raika
- Ikazuchi Raiga
- Ikazuchi Nariko
- Ikazuchi Arashi
- Ikazuchi Sorae
- Ikazuchi Raito

Current role note:
- Rin and Ruther train with the Elven royal family, and Masako is a major reason they remain there safely.

=====================================
RAIFU STEP
=====================================

Origin:
- Created by Tomoe and Hayate

Core formula:
- electric Qana = acceleration
- wind Qana = motion control
- body Qana = survival

True Raifu Step is restricted to:
- Tomoe
- Hayate
- virgin male descendants of House Ikazuchi still under Mother Qana contract

Female descendants and non-virgin male descendants may only use lesser unstable burst forms.

It is movement through space, not teleportation.
Leg and lower-body injuries can disable safe use.

House saying:
- Break the legs, stop the storm.

=====================================

=====================================
KAGUTSUME BRANCH FAMILY

HOUSE SORYEONG — DARK ELF RULING CLAN
=====================================

Identity:
- ruling Dark Elf clan family
- center of Moon-Seal and deathcraft authority
- follows Death Dragon teachings rather than Mother Qana river contract norms

Leadership exception:
- House Soryeong is led by a male clan head

Current clan head:
- Soryeong Mu-jin
  - elderly male head
  - former terrifying assassin-lord
  - now gentle but deeply feared funeral-father figure

Public rumor about Mu-jin's wife:
- she disappeared after battle with Severence

Secret truth:
- she was captured by Severence while pregnant
- the fetus was kept alive artificially
- that fetus became Oren
- later, Dragon Jotunn was forced into baby Oren

Children of Mu-jin:
- Soryeong Ji-hoon
- Soryeong Hye-jin
- Soryeong Eun-bi

Dark Elf suborders:
- Soryeong Paksu
  - male death-workers and soul-rite specialists
- Saja Blades
  - female assassins, Lunacron hunters, and death-warrant enforcers

Internal rule:
- the men guide the dead
- the women enforce death

=====================================
WIND ELF EXCEPTION
=====================================

- Wind Elves are not a standard noble house.
- They are nomadic, pacifist, and tied to the Time Dragon.
- Hayate is a Wind Elf who remained in the Elven Kingdom through love and marriage rather than ordinary clan structure.
