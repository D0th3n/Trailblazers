TRAILBLAZERS — MOGLIM BIOLOGY

Name: Moglim
Type: Small rock-based creature

Size:
Approximately the size of a yoga ball.

Structure:
Outer shell made of cracked stone.
Inner core is soft, organic, and slightly glowing.

Movement:
Rolls and hops using small hidden legs.

Behavior:
Low intelligence, instinct-driven.
Often found in groups.

Attack Mechanism:
Heats its rocky exterior using internal Qana.
Launches itself at targets like a living meteor.

Ecological Role:
Often found near larger Qana anomalies such as Titan-class golems.
Acts as a byproduct of environmental instability.

Threat Level:
Low individually, dangerous in groups.
