title: "Welcome to the Trailblazers archive"
---

This is the first public post for **Trailblazers**, a place to document the game's universe as it takes shape.

The goal of this site is simple:

- publish lore in a way that feels intentional and exciting
- keep a public record of development progress
- share images, concept art, and screenshots as the world grows

As the project evolves, this archive can expand into a full codex with dedicated pages for factions, characters, locations, and major story arcs.
